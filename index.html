Welcome to the maZe of the undead. Beware that once you enter you will die.....eventually You are the lone survivor in this mad world and all you have to defend yourself with is your pistol These Zombies are not like the slow moving walkers from the walking dead, no these zombies are predators

And you are their prey.....

You are currently trapped in a maze. There is ammo, health, and burgers scattered about the map Your Health will always slowly heal as well as your stamina for sprinting. Eat more burgers to run for extended periods of time or try and wait for your sprint bar to raise up. you will need it, these zombies are fast! You should always shoot zombies coming your way but be mindful of ammo. the careless player will run out of ammo then stamina trying to run away and finally health as the zombies overtake you. Careful players will take careful shots and use the walls as their advantage blocking zombies from your body. Be careful in the maze but know this. Zombies can drop supplies and health and in the rare occasion a zombie may even have a map or a gps to help you find your way out. Get both unless you wish to guaruntee your demise.

Now about the game.....

Theres only one way out and its the farthest point away from where you start, the bottom right corner of the maZe. When you reach the end you may click and begin another round but the maze will grow as your sanity fades there is no true end to the game, you simply try and clear as many mazelevels as possible until you can no longer survive.

Controls:

Movement:

Move Up: W

Move Left: A

Move Down: S

Move Right: D

Sprint: Hold: Left-Shift

Shooting:

Shoot: Left-Click or Space

Aim: Aim with Mouse
